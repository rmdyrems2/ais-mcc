ACCOUNTING INFORMATION SYSTEM

Project Context

The purpose of this study is to explain and understand the used of accounting information management system in the transaction process the billing and invoice, due the human error in calculation of the transactions we will propose this project to have a accurate and efficient calculation of the transaction process using the Accounting Information System(AIS).

These are the transactions that will be made in the school by the students who will pay their bills, For Regular Students with Unifast,once the students will undergoing an assessment slip he/she must have a study load to be calculated of there payments the cashier will assist them and then after the cashier received the study load, cashier will calculate the tuition fees and other expenses through the issued slip, The payments indicated are the following, Names of a student ,Tuition fees heading ,Undergrad with units,MISCFEES,Entrancefee,Caltural/SportsMed/Dental,Computer/TechfeeID,Insuranc e Community. ExtnProjects Other Charges, and Total. After they received the bill of their Assessment Slip the Unifast will shoulder all the bill of a student with Unifast accredited. Every student has a budget of the government with the amount of twenty thousand pesos, and the student will procced to Enrolment process.

For Graduating Student with a Unifast, Once the graduating students will undergoing an assessment slip he/she must have a study load to be calculated of there payments the cashier will assist them and then after the cashier received the study load, cashier will calculate the tuition fees and other expenses through the issued slip, The payments indicated are the following :Names of a student, Tuition feesheading,Undergrad with units,MISC FEES,Entrance fee, Lab, Caltural/Sports Med/Dental,Computer/Tech, feeID,Insurance Community Extn,Projects, Other Charges,Diploma,Certification ,Transfer Certificate ,Others,Total. After they received the bill of their Assessment Slip the Unifast will shoulder all the bill of a student with Unifast accredited. Every student has a budget of the government with the amount of twenty thousand pesos, and the student will procced to Scheduled Ceremony Graduation and have the TOR and Diploma.
