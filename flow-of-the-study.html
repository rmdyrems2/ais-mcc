ACCOUNTING INFORMATION SYSTEM



Flow of the study

AIMS is a system or set of processes for collecting data about accounting transactions; recording, organizing, and summarizing the data; and culminating with the preparation of financial statements and other reports for internal and external users. The AIMS drives to improve the current accounting processes in MCC . The system ensures the convenience of managing accounting information.
