ACCOUNTING INFORMATION SYSTEM

ABSTRACT

Recently there are many businesses and companies are using manual accounting that can cause a high-cost and expensive expenses due to human error and using a paper to manually records their transaction, especially the billing and invoice that is more important of an accounting of the company and the businesses, this problems can be become an inefficient to the company and Double-entry accounting in a manual system is laborious, since every transaction must be recorded by hand, twice. Many accounting programs use a double-entry method, but second entry is created automatically. While this wonâ€™t stop a wrong number from being entered, it does eliminate discrepancies between the first and second entries, so that by manually recording of the billing and invoice can be more inaccurate to all businesses. In our group we are proposing the computerized transaction that can avoid human errors and can minimize the use of papers that can be use in the accounting, this project is part of Accounting Information System (AIS) the billing and invoice.

The project will be focus in billing and Invoicing and application for different business purposes helps mainly the service providers and freelancers to manage, send professional invoices online, and track its status. Generally, the school in Mandaue City College facing a various issue for managing and tracking the invoice status of students, which mostly back to the lacks of adapting new technology in these school. One of these lacks is tracking the bills status for a definite project. Therefore, this research intends to design and develop an computerized billing and invoice system that are part of Accounting Information System (AIS) to expenses effortlessly and saves both time and money on the employees in the Mandaue City College (MCC). The propose system intend to provide a digital tracking of the time spend on projects and send invoices directly to client or students. An evaluation was conducted among the accounting employees and administrators at Mandaue City College to perceive their opinion towards the system ease of use, usefulness, and satisfaction.
